Brownies Recipe

Description

A foolproof brownie recipe for a squidgy chocolate bake. Makes about 20

Equipment Required

  • Shallow baking tin, approximately 20 x 25 cm
  • Mixing bowl
  • Whisk
  • Weighing scales
  • Large spoon
  • Oven

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter, cubed (250 g)
  • Dark chocolate, about 70%, broken into pieces (250 g)
  • Medium eggs (3)
  • Caster sugar (275 g)
  • Self-raising flour (125 g)
  • Vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • Salt (a pinch)

Method

  1. Grease the baking tin and line with baking paper.
  2. Set the oven at 180 Â°C/Gas Mark 4.
  3. Melt the butter and chocolate in a heatproof bowl, using the oven whilst it warms up to temperature.
  4. Whisk the eggs, sugar, and vanilla essence together in a large bowl until combined.
  5. Beat the melted chocolate and butter mixture in with the eggs.
  6. Combine the salt with the flour and sift them over the chocolate mixture.
  7. Gently fold the flour in with a large spoon, being careful not to over-work the mixture.
  8. Pour the mixture into the prepared tin, and smooth the top with a spatula.
  9. Bake for 20 - 25 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out with a few moist crumbs clinging on. Don't be tempeted to overcook them!
