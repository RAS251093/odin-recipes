Scones

Description

These scones are the perfect treat for a summertime afternoon tea! Eat them plain, with butter, or topped with jam and clotted cream (controversial but... in whichever order you like).

Equipment Required

  • Baking tray, lined with baking paper
  • 6 cm round cutter
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Measuring jug
  • Weighing scales
  • Oven

Ingredients

  • Self raising flour (250 g)
  • Caster sugar (50 g)
  • Unsalted butter, chilled, cubed (100 g)
  • Medium egg (1)
  • Milk (100 mL)
  • Salt (a pinch)

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 °C/200 °C fan/Gas 7
