Pizza Recipe

Description

Even a novice cook can master the art of pizza with this simple step-by-step guide. Bellissimo!

Equipment Required

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Measuring jug
  • Baking tray
  • Rolling pin
  • Oven

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • Strong bread flour (300 g)
  • Instant yeast (1 tsp)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp plus extra for drizzling)

Tomato Sauce

  • Passata (100 mL)
  • Fresh basil (handful)
  • Garlic (1 clove, crushed)

Pizza Topping

  • Mozzarella (125 g)
  • Parmesan, grated (handful)
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved (handful)
  • Fresh basil, optional (handful)

Method

Making the Pizza Dough

  1. Mix the flour, yeast and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Make a well in the flour and pour in warm water (200 mL), and the olive oil.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients in with a spoon until you have a soft, fairly wet dough.
  4. Turn the wet dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 10 mins until smooth.
  5. Place the dough into a clean, oiled, bowl and cover with a tea towel.
  6. Set the bowl aside and allow to rise until doubled in size.

Making the Tomato Sauce

  1. Mix together the passata, basil and crushed garlic.
  2. Season to taste and leave to stand at room temperature.

Bringing it all Together

  1. Heat the oven to 240 °C/220 °C fan/gas mark 8.
  2. Place an upturned baking tray in the oven on the top shelf and allow the warm.
  3. Give the dough a quick knead, then split into two balls.
  4. On a floured surface, roll out the dough into large rounds, about 25 cm across, using a rolling pin. The dough needs to be very thin as it will rise in the oven.
  5. Lift the rounds onto two floured baking trays.
  6. Top the bases with tomatoe sauce, mozzarella, and tomatoes. Drizzle with olvie oil, and season.
  7. Put the pizzas on top of the preheated baking tray, one at a time, baking for 8 - 10 mins until crisp.
  8. Serve with a little more olive oil, and basil leaves if desired.
  9. Repeat for the remaining pizza.
